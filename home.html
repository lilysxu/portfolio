Hi there! Welcome to my personal website, coded and designed from scratch by yours truly ;) I decided to create a website after half a year of toying around in Canva, creating designs with nowhere to display them. But this isn’t just a portfolio – this is a culmination and celebration of my life through a different lens. Everything you see here means something to me. Yes, even that bow and that pencil. As you click through each page, I’d like you to try and find meaning in each item to piece together my story. They say a picture is worth a thousand words, so I’ll leave your intepretation of what everything means up to you – for art is subjective, and so is life. Thanks for stopping by.
