About

Hey there! My name’s Lily and I’m a rising senior based in the United States. To be honest, when I first started creating this website I viewed technology as simply a means to display art: it only existed in my mind as a tool and could never be art itself. I slowly realized I was looking at everything wrong as my website came to a close, however. In each line of code laid a vision of mine - when I caught myself thinking, 'the timing of this text fade has to be exactly 4.5 seconds' or 'the placement of that button needed margins of 50 pixels or else everything will fail' it felt a whole lot like I was designing another graphic or painting another piece, where every brushstroke was meticulously placed. Technology - websites, games, software - all exists with intent behind it, with the desire to bring emotion and meaning to others' lives, and isn't that precisely what art is too? I'm glad I was able to create this website and get to know technology a little better: so now, it's your turn to get to know me.
